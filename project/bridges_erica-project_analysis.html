Fat Wreck Chords | Project Analysis

  • Project: Re-Design
  • Web Designer: Erica Bridges

Site Intent

The primary focus of this re-deign is to eliminate visual confusion and make the site more user friendly and aesthetically pleasing. As it stands, the site applies harsh color combinations and clutter. It is very difficult to identify the logo as it clashes with the picture background of a band member.

Site re-design will be accomplished by applying a navigation that runs across the top of the page, as opposed to the current site that applies a break between each link. Also, by combining the media content (ie. Videos, Records, MP3s & Pics) into one drop down menu, it will make for a more organized navigation. The re-design would also need to incorporate responsive design.

Competitive Analysis

Based on the research that was preformed, the site Fat Wreck Chords, has very few visitors. Fat Wreck Chords is an independent record label for punk bands. According to Alexa.com, Fat Wreck Chordsis ranked at 333,417. The site itself has been online for fourteen years and has a relatively good traffic rank. The target audience for this site is mostly males from the age of 25-34 yrs., have no children, received some college education and view the site mostly from home. Within the last month, the traffic amount has decreased by 29%. and user only stay on the site on average for about two minutes spending 30 seconds on each page. This is possibley due to the design of the site itself. The site needs to maintain the target audience, however, create more white space and eye trails to keep the user interested. Targeted Audience

The Line Jumpers

Band of five young guys. The lead singer works for a record store in the Bay Area and has heard about Fat Wreck Chords. The band has been working on their songs and has finally been able to create a demo to send to the record company to hopefully get signed. The band, collectively has looked into other labels, yet has not made a decision yet. The want a record label that allows the band to stay creative and push the envelope. The band has come up with a lot of questions and want to make sure that the record label is easy to get in contact with or least have FAQ's that can help. The Line Jumpers are also concerned about wether or not the marketing of the record label is done well.

Velvet Lizards

Band of three guys out of New York. Have already signed with the label and visit the site often to make sure their record and the bands merchandise is being marketing well to the public. The Lizards also have a gig coming up and have concerns about the advertisement of the concert and make sure the venues directions are listed.
